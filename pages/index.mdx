# Introduction
 
Welcome to Polylearn! This is the place to learn everything from the basics of python to advanced deep learning! Depending on your level or current class, click on one of the desired modules.


Modules include <strong>Intro to Python</strong>, <strong>Python Intermediate</strong> and <strong>Machine Learning</strong>.

Click on your desired **module folder** to begin! Email us at polylearn4@gmail.com for any issues, concerns or any thoughts to make our program better!
## What is Polylearn?

We make learning Python and ML **simple**, **accessible** and **fun** for middle and high school students. Learn more at **Polylearn.org**
